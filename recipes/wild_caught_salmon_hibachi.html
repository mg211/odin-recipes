Wild Caught Salmon Hibachi

Ingredients:

  • 2 wild-caught salmon fillets
  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, onions, zucchini)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Grill the Salmon:

    2. Season the salmon fillets with salt and black pepper. Grill or pan-sear them until cooked to your liking.

  2. Stir-Fry Vegetables:

    3. In a separate pan, stir-fry the mixed vegetables with minced garlic and soy sauce until tender.

  3. Combine and Serve:

    4. Serve the grilled salmon on a bed of jasmine rice, topped with the stir-fried vegetables. Add a pat of butter for extra flavor.
