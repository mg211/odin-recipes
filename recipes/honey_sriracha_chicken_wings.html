Honey Sriracha Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup sriracha sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Wings:

    2. In a bowl, mix sriracha sauce, honey, soy sauce, and minced garlic. Toss the chicken wings in the marinade and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Bake or Grill:

    3. Preheat your oven or grill to 400°F (200°C). Bake or grill the wings for about 25-30 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through and crispy.

  3. Garnish and Serve:

    4. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the wings for garnish, and serve hot.
