Brick Oven Double Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup mini pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    2. Preheat your brick oven to 500°F (260°C) or as hot as it can go.

  2. Prepare the Pizza Dough:

    3. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

  3. Assemble the Pizza:

    4. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza peel or an inverted baking sheet dusted with cornmeal. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.

    Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add the sliced pepperoni and mini pepperoni on top of the cheese.

  4. Bake in the Brick Oven:

    5. Transfer the pizza to the preheated brick oven. Bake for about 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  5. Remove and Garnish:

    6. Using the pizza peel, remove the pizza from the oven. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, and garlic powder over the hot pizza. Add red pepper flakes if you like it spicy. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

  6. Serve:

    7. Allow the pizza to cool for a minute or two, then slice and serve hot.
