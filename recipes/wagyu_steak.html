Wagyu Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 Wagyu steak cuts
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. Season the Steak:

    2. Season the Wagyu steaks generously with salt and black pepper.

  2. Sear the Steak:

    3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over high heat. Add minced garlic and rosemary sprigs. Sear the steaks for about 2-3 minutes per side for medium-rare, or longer to your desired doneness.

  3. Rest and Serve:

    4. Remove the steaks from the skillet, add butter, and let the steaks rest for a few minutes. Slice and serve.
